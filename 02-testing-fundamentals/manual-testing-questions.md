## Q1. What is Software Testing?

**Answer:**  
Software testing is the process of verifying and validating a software application to ensure it meets the specified requirements and to identify defects before it is released to end users.

## Q2. What is the difference between Verification and Validation?

**Answer:**

Verification means checking whether we are building the product correctly according to the given requirements and design documents. It usually involves activities like reviews, walkthroughs, and inspections before actual testing starts. In simple terms, it answers the question: “Are we building the product right?”

Validation means checking whether the final developed product actually meets the customer’s needs and works as expected. This involves executing the software through different types of testing. It answers the question: “Are we building the right product?”
